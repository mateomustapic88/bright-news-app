## Google Play

### Suggested app name

`BrightNews`

### Suggested short description

`Credible positive news from around the world without the usual doomscrolling.`

### Suggested full description

`BrightNews is a calmer way to stay informed.

Instead of endless outrage and doomscrolling, BrightNews highlights credible uplifting stories from around the world. The app focuses on real progress, human impact, and meaningful wins across environment, science, health, community, animals, and innovation.

What you can do in BrightNews:

- Read positive stories from multiple global regions
- Save stories to your account and sync them across devices
- Explore categories like environment, science, health, community, animals, and innovation
- Open original source links for every story
- Sign in with Google for a simple beta experience

BrightNews is built for readers who still want signal, context, and real reporting, but do not want their news habit dominated by fear, rage, and noise.

The app is still in beta and will continue improving its sourcing, moderation, and account tools over time.`
