Account Deletion Request

BrightNews users can request deletion of their app account and associated app data through this web resource. This page exists to satisfy web-based account deletion access for users who may not be inside the app.

How to request deletion

  1. Email the BrightNews support contact listed below.
  2. Use the subject line: BrightNews account deletion request.
  3. Include the email address used for your BrightNews account.

Replace this placeholder before store submission with the real BrightNews support email or deletion form URL.

Deletion contact placeholder: support@brightnews.app

What BrightNews deletes

  • Account profile information stored for BrightNews app use.
  • Saved-story records associated with the account.
  • Other app-linked account data that is not required to be retained for legal or security reasons.

Possible retention exceptions

BrightNews may retain limited information for a reasonable period where required for fraud prevention, security, legal compliance, or dispute handling.
